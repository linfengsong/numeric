So, \(7^{999} \equiv 7 \pmod{8}\).

### Step 3: Use the Chinese Remainder Theorem

x \equiv 7 \pmod{8}, \quad x \equiv 18 \pmod{125}.

125k + 18 \equiv 7 \pmod{8}.
