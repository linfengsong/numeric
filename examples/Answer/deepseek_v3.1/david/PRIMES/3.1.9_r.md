### (a) Primes that are 1 more than a power of 2

- \( k = 1 \): \( 2^1 + 1 = 3 \) (prime)

- \( k = 3 \): \( 2^3 + 1 = 9 \) (not prime)
- \( k = 4 \): \( 2^4 + 1 = 17 \) (prime)
- \( k = 5 \): \( 2^5 + 1 = 33 \) (not prime)
- \( k = 6 \): \( 2^6 + 1 = 65 \) (not prime)
- \( k = 7 \): \( 2^7 + 1 = 129 \) (not prime)
- \( k = 8 \): \( 2^8 + 1 = 257 \) (prime)

So, two more primes of this form (besides 5) are:
- \( 3 = 2^1 + 1 \)
- \( 17 = 2^4 + 1 \)

**Answer:**
