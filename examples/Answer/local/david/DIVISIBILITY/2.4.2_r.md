 (a) We have
\begin{align*}
72 &= 1 \cdot 56 + 16, \\
56 &= 3 \cdot 16 + 8, \\
16 &= 2 \cdot 8 + 0.
\end{align*}
So, gcd(56, 72) = 8. Now we work backwards to find x and y:
\begin{align*}
8 &= 56 - 3 \cdot 16 \\
&= 56 - 3(72 - 1 \cdot 56) \\
&= 4 \cdot 56 - 3 \cdot 72.
\end{align*}
So, x = 4 and y = -3.

(b) We have
\begin{align*}
138 &= 5 \cdot 24 + 18, \\
24 &= 1 \cdot 18 + 6, \\
18 &= 3 \cdot 6 + 0.
\end{align*}
So, gcd(24, 138) = 6. Now we work backwards to find x and y:
\begin{align*}
6 &= 24 - 1 \cdot 18 \\
&= 24 - 1(138 - 5 \cdot 24) \\
&= 6 \cdot 24 - 1 \cdot 138.
\end{align*}
So, x = 6 and y = -1.

(c) We have
\begin{align*}
272 &= 2 \cdot 119 + 34, \\
119 &= 3 \cdot 34 + 11, \\
34 &= 3 \cdot 11 + 1, \\
11 &= 11 \cdot 1 + 0.
\end{align*}
So, gcd(119, 272) = 1. Now we work backwards to find x and y:
\begin{align*}
1 &= 34 - 3 \cdot 11 \\
&= 34 - 3(119 - 3 \cdot 34) \\
&= 10 \cdot 34 - 3 \cdot 119 \\
&= 10(272 - 2 \cdot 119) - 3 \cdot 119 \\
&= 10 \cdot 272 - 23 \cdot 119.
\end{align*}
So, x = 10 and y = -23.

2378 &= 1 \cdot 1769 + 609, \\

551 &= 9 \cdot 58 + 29, \\
