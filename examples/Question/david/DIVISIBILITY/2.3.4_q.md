For n $\geq{1}$, use mathematical induction to establish each of the following divisibility statements:
   (a) $8 | 5^{2n} + 7$. 
       [Hint: $5^{2(k+l)} + 7 = 5^2(5^{2k} + 7) + (7 - 5^2 \cdot 7)$.]
   (b) $15 | 2^{4n} - 1$.
   (c) $5 | 3^{3n+l} + 2^{n+1}$.
   (d) $21 | 4^{n+l}+ 5^{2n-1}$.
   (e) $24 | 2 \cdot 7^n + 3 \cdot 5^n - 5$.
